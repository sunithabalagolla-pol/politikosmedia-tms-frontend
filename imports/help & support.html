Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

To add a new team member, navigate to the "Team Members" section under MANAGEMENT in the sidebar. Click the "Add Member" button in the top right, fill out their details, and assign them to a department.

You can create a new task by clicking the "New Task" button from the Overview or Tasks page. Fill in the title, description, assignees, and set a due date before saving.

The Kanban board allows you to drag and drop tasks between different status columns (e.g., To Do, In Progress, Done). Access it from the OVERVIEW section in the sidebar.

Go to the "Reports" section under MANAGEMENT. Select the date range and type of report you need, then click "Generate". You can export the results as PDF or CSV.

Navigate to your "Profile" under the SYSTEM section. Click on "Security" and follow the prompts to change your password.

Go to "Settings" under the SYSTEM section, then select the "Notifications" tab to customize your email and in-app notification preferences.
